About

One Iris is written by Chris Luo, an undergraduate student at Brown University. The blog began the summer of 2014 as a way for Chris to organize new thoughts and reflections as his college years began. Since then, One Iris has grown to include short articles on a variety of topics and experimental writing. Ultimately, the blog aims to find depth and meaning in unexpected places.

Chris studies computer science at university, along with an assortment of humanities courses. In his major, Chris enjoys general Software Engineering and frontend Programming. When he's not doing coursework or writing words/code, he enjoys diving in the Youtubic Ocean, teasing his family's dog, or playing/analyzing videogames way too deeply. Also he thinks that if he were born 1,000 years ago, he would be a mountain hermit.
